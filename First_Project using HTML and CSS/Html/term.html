    Booking Procedure

    Car rental booking is now made easier with our new reservation system, providing real-time availability information, online payment and instant confirmation. 1/ Check availability - select Pick-Up Date & Location, Return Date & Location, enter your age, and you will see a list of available vehicles and prices. 2/ Select the vehicle of your choice. Press the “Book Now” button. 3/ Choose rental options (if any), add discount coupon (if applicable), fill in driver details. Check the box, confirming that you have read the Terms and Conditions and then press “Confirm Booking”. A summary of your booking with payment details will appear. Please review the details and if all is in order, press “Confirm Booking”.

    4/ Complete the payment information. The system navigates to a ‘Thank you’ page and sends you an automatic email listing all the services you booked. Your booking is confirmed and the vehicle is reserved if payment has been completed successfully. Important note: Reservations without completed payment are still processed but considered pending. Within 12 hours of receipt of the pending reservation we will contact you to clarify details and payment options. Please note, however, for unpaid reservations type of vehicle and price may change subject to availability.

    Genral Conditions

    The car rental is subject to the terms of the Car Rental Agreement executed between the Renter and Renaissance Rent a Car at the time of the car pick-up.

    Pricing

    Pricing is quoted on a ‘per day’ basis (24 hour period). Our inclusive rates include collision damage waiver (CDW), fire and theft waiver (FTW), third party liability (TPL), road tax, unlimited mileage, additional driver, VAT

    Vechicle condition

    The vehicle must be returned to the drop-off location specified in the contract in the same condition as when it was picked up by the renter. It is the responsibility of the renter to ensure that the vehicle is thoroughly checked prior to picking up the vehicle. The client accepts responsibility for taking due care and attention of the vehicle. Failure to do so and loss or damage to the interior and exterior of the vehicle is deemed negligent and responsible for the charges as listed in the damage matrix. Whilst in charge of the vehicle the client accepts responsibility for maintaining the roadworthiness of the vehicle and agrees to take remedial action to avoid unnecessary cost/damage. This will include maintaining oil levels, tire pressures and other common sense actions to maintain the vehicle. Should a cost is involved, the required action needs to be communicated with the rental company.

    Cancellation Policy

    Cancellation of your reservation should be made by email or text/viber/whatsapp message at least 48 hours prior to the start of your rental. Please note that telephone cancellations will not be accepted.

    Cancellation charges

    There are no cancellation charges for bookings cancelled up to 48 hours prior to rental. For bookings cancelled less than 48 hours of rental, the cost of 1 day rental is due.

    Presonal and Driving Requirements

    The required minimum age to rent a vehicle is 25 years for SUV vehicles, and 21 years for all other vehicles. Customers must present at the time of rental: a valid driving license, issued at least 2 years prior to the day of rental, passport, and a credit card. Personal and driving requirements apply to all additional drivers.

    Payment Method

    We accept payment by cash, bank transfer, credit or debit cards. We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa and Mastercard). American Express and Diners are not accepted.

    BreakDown/Accident

    In the event of a breakdown or accident, the driver must contact Renaissance Rent A Car immediately for instructions and assistance. It is highly recommended to call the police even for the smallest of damages. A protocol from the police, car documents and keys must be presented for the insurance to be activated. If the driver fails to act accordingly, s/he may be charged for all damages incurred as a result. If an accident is caused by a driver under the influence of alcohol or drugs, or in any other circumstances in which the insurance coverage is excluded, the driver shall be responsible for all the damages incurred to the car rental company.

    Fuel

    We operate “full-to-full” fuel policy, which means that all cars are delivered with full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank of fuel from a petrol station no more than 10 km from the car rental branch. Proof (receipt) is required when the car is returned. If the car tank is not full, a refueling charge of 15 EUR applies in addition to the cost of the missing fuel.

    Unlimited Millage

    Unlimited mileage is included in the rates for all cars.
